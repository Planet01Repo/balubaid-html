FURNITURE LEADERS

Effective Furnishing Solutions

Furniture Leaders was founded in 1999 by Sheikh Mohamed Balubaid, and his brother Sheikh Ali Balubaid (may God have mercy on them), pushed by their genuine sense of belonging to the Arabic & Islamic culture and art, and endless passion towards interior design and its aesthetics. Within a short time, it turned to be one of the leading commercial brands in the Saudi office furniture market, providing the highly demanded creative and sustainable workspace solutions that effectively contributed to the great and fast development of the business landscape. Furniture leaders started with a small team of ambitious employees who formed a perfect work environment flowing with love, cooperation, and determination to perfection and enriching their experience by learning and gaining knowledge all the time. The company experienced several changes that helped it turn into one of the largest furniture and interior design companies in the gulfarea that provides unique, comprehensive furnishing solutions suiting all tastes and levels. The company adhered to balance design and fulfilling market needs by providing advanced, modern design services that match the clients brand image, giving them a personalized touch and a unique visual identity

AWTAD

Awtad is a fit out furniture company specializes in creating and supplying high-quality custom-made furniture for both commercial and residential spaces. The company has a wide range of products available, ranging from contemporary and classic styles to modern and rustic designs. Awtad offer a range of finishes, including wood, metal and glass, and they also provide a bespoke service to create custom pieces to perfectly match their customers' needs. In addition to their furniture range, Awtad also provides a full fit-out service, providing furniture and fittings to transform any space. The company works with commercial, residential and hospitality clients to design and install furniture that meets their exact requirements. Awtad has a team of experienced designers and craftsmen to create innovative, functional and beautiful furniture that is tailor-made to the client's needs. Overall, Awtad is a company that offers high-quality custom-made furniture for both commercial and residential spaces, along with a full fit-out service to transform any space. With experienced designers and craftsmen, Awtad can create unique and beautiful pieces that perfectly match their clients' needs.
