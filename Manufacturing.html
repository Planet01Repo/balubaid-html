Balubaid Manufacturing

Balubaid Group diversified into the manufacturing of light industrial products in 1995, today it operates two manufacturing companies (Arabian Plastic Industrial Co. and Arabian Plant for Lubricants & Greases)

Arabian Plastic Industrial Company Limited

The company is known for developing and producing high quality products trusted by the most discerning customers in the Kingdom.
Arabian Plastic Industrial Company Limited (APICO) was established in 1996. APICO provides superior Injection Molding production services, as well as high-quality Extrusion of Films, Blow Molding and Thermoforming Disposable products. Our activities are based on a set of values and ethical principles such as Honesty, Integrity, Transparency, Reliability, Efficiency, Innovation and Quality.

Arabian Plant for Lubricants & Grease

Previously known as Salamah Lubricants, APLG was established in 1996 offering a wide range of automotive and industrial lubricants and greases. APLG produces antifreeze fluid and automatic transmission oil and is also involved in blending of engine oil. It handles auto care and industrial lubricants that are widely sold in the auto market and to other suppliers. As part of its historic legacy, APLG has worked closely with Shell, Mobil, Valvoline, Petromin and Qbrex.
APLG’s products play a vital role in the functioning of numerous foundational Saudi industries like Saudi Arabian Airlines, Saudi Electric Power Generation Company, Saudi Water Desalination, as well as leading cement manufacturers, construction corporations and food processing factories.
City: Jeddah
Address: 2 Al Iad Ar Riyadi, Industrial Area
