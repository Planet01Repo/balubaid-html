Real Estate

Founded in 1979, Balubaid Real Estate is a leading property management and development firm, specializing in the ownership and management of premier shopping malls in the western region of Saudi Arabia, featuring a diverse array of local and international brands. The division is engaged in the development, construction, sales, leasing, and management of commercial offices and properties. Among the well-known shopping malls owned and managed by Balubaid Real Estate are Al Hijaz Mall Makkah, Al Mousadia Plaza, Al Hijaz Mall Jeddah I and Al Hijaz Mall Jeddah II

HIJAZ MALL MAKKAH

Developed in 1998 on 28,000 sq.m of land size, and located on the main Makkah highway to Jeddah. Hijaz Makkah covers 16,000 sq.m of GLA (Gross Leasable Area) and includes 250 units of local and international brands into two levels, with a massive car-parking space. Hijaz Mall Makkah is one of the best family shopping destinations in the holy city of Makkah. Hijaz Mall Makkah is also home to Jarir Bookstore, CityMax, MAC, DAMAS, Tim Hortons, Starbucks, Route 66, Skechers, Giordano, and other local brands with a large specialized food court.

MUSADIA PLAZA

Musadia Plaza is one of the most successful Strip mall concepts in Jeddah city, and located on prime property, near the intersection of Madinah Road and Palestine Street. Musadia Mall has been a hub for famous wedding fashion designers, perfumes, carpets, cosmetics, evening dresses, electronic appliances with a variety of a well selected International brands.

HIJAZ MALL JEDDAH

Hijaz Mall Jeddah is an established community mall in the Bawadi Dist. Of Jeddah, developed in phases between 1988 and 1992. Hijaz Jeddah offers 1500 sq.m of GLA, housing over 350 units of local and international brands on two levels. Notable tenants includes Baskin Robins, Naomi, Body Shop. Hijaz Mall Jeddah is trusted by families providing a safe, enjoyable, and pleasant shopping experience.
