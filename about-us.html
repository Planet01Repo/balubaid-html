About UsBalubaid Group of Companies (BGC) is a diversified holding company operating in eight sectors in Saudi Arabia. Established in 1952 Starting as a spare parts trading operation, the company has grown into a multi-business enterprise with interests in automotive retail, industrial and service equipment, transportation, real estate, financial services, manufacturing, and hospitality. BGC is a privately held, family-owned enterprise with a diverse, multinational workforce of over 1000. The company has also been a pioneer in institutionalizing governance practices in the Middle East and continues to expand its activities and corporate culture
