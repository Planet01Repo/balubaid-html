NATIONAL FINANCE HOUSE

National Finance House Company (“NFH”) is a Closed Joint Stock Company that operates under the licensing and regulation of the Saudi Central Bank (SAMA). The company follows Shariah principles in its financing activities. NFH was established in Jeddah in 2021 with a Paid-up Capital of 100 million Saudi Riyals. In January 2023, The Company increased its Paid-up Capital to 150 million Saudi Riyals. The Company received a license from SAMA in June 2021 to engage in the activities of financial leasing, consumer financing, and financing for small and medium-sized enterprises.
